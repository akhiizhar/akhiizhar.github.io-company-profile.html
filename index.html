About Us

Hotel Grand Kenari Karawang is a premier destination for both business and leisure travelers. Located in the bustling city of Karawang, our hotel offers a perfect blend of modern amenities and traditional Indonesian hospitality.

With our spacious rooms, state-of-the-art facilities, and dedicated staff, we strive to make your stay as comfortable and memorable as possible. Whether you're here for a business meeting or a relaxing vacation, Hotel Grand Kenari Karawang is your home away from home.
